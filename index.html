Forex

The largest currency trading market in the world is the foreign exchange market, or Forex. It enables you to take advantage of currency rate variations by buying and selling currencies, such as euros for US dollars.

Currencies are exchanged on the over-the-counter (OTC) market five days a week, 24 hours a day. It's one of the biggest trading marketplaces, with over $5 trillion in worldwide turnover per day.

Crypto

Crypto refers to digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security. They work on decentralized networks based on blockchain technology, providing a safe and transparent means to transmit and store value.

Crypto trading is the purchasing and selling of digital currencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. Traders seek to profit from price fluctuations in these extremely volatile markets, utilizing innovative tools and tactics for the best results.

read more
CFDs

CFDs are a sort of financial instrument that allows you to speculate on the price movements of assets such as stocks, currencies, and commodities without actually owning them. You can benefit whether the price rises or falls, but it is dangerous because losses are multiplied.

Trade CFDs on a wide selection of popular stocks, commodities, and currencies.
